# Sector-Specific Threat Landscapes

## 1. Orientation

### What this module covers
Different industries face different threats. A Hospital fears Ransomware (Life safety). A Defense Contractor fears Espionage (IP Theft). A Bank fears Fraud.

### Learning Objectives
*   Profile the threat landscape for **Finance, Healthcare, Energy, Retail**.
*   Understand **Vertical-specific** vectors (SWIFT for banks, ICS for energy).

---

## 2. Core Content

### 1. Finance (Banks)
*   *Threat:* ATM Cashouts, SWIFT hacks (North Korea), Wire Fraud (BEC).
*   *Actor:* Sophisticated Crime (Carbanak).
*   *Defense:* Fraud detection, anomaly detection.

### 2. Healthcare (Hospitals)
*   *Threat:* Ransomware (LockBit).
*   *Leverage:* "Pay or patients die."
*   *Defense:* Continuity of Operations (Paper records), Segmentation of IoT devices.

### 3. Energy (Oil/Gas/Electric)
*   *Threat:* Sabotage (Sandworm).
*   *Vector:* OT/ICS (Operational Technology).
*   *Defense:* Air-gapping, Purdue Model.

### 4. Retail
*   *Threat:* POS (Point of Sale) RAM Scraping.
*   *Goal:* Credit Card numbers (millions).
*   *Defense:* P2PE (Point to point encryption).
