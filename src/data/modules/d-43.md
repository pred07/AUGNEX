# Mental Health and Burnout in Cyber

## 1. Orientation

### What this module covers
This module covers **Sustainability**. Cyber has one of the highest burnout rates of any industry.

### Fit in the Learning Path
You can't defend the network if you are exhausted. This is a survival skill.

### Learning Objectives
By the end of this module, you should understand:
*   **Alert Fatigue** (Psychological impact).
*   **Imposter Syndrome**.
*   **Disconnecting**.

---

## 2. Core Content

### The Causes
1.  **Always On**: Attacks happen 24/7/365.
2.  **High Stakes**: One mistake implies disaster.
3.  **Gatekeeping**: "You don't know Assembly? You aren't a real hacker." (Toxic culture).

### Imposter Syndrome
"Everyone else is a genius; I'm a fraud."
*   *Reality*: The field is too big. No one knows everything. Everyone googles things.

---

## 3. Guided Practice

### Coping Strategies
1.  **Boundaries**: Don't check Slack after hours.
2.  **Community**: Talk to peers. Realize they function the same way.
3.  **Hobbies**: Do something analog. Woodworking. Hiking. Touch grass.

---

## 4. Reflection Check

1.  **Hero Complex**: "I have to fix it all myself." (This leads to burnout. trust your team).
2.  **Perfectionism**: Security is never done. It is never perfect. Accept "Good Enough for Now".
3.  **Management**: Good managers protect their team from the chaos.

---

## 5. Completion Criteria

This module is complete when:
1.  You admit that **Imposter Syndrome** is normal.
2.  You set one boundary for your digital life.
3.  You understand that Health > Uptime.
