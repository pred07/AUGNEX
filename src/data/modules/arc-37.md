# Neurodiversity in Cyber

## 1. Orientation

### What this module covers
This module covers **The Superpower**. ADHD, Autism, and Pattern Recognition.

### Fit in the Learning Path
Cybersecurity attracts neurodivergent minds. Leading them requires understanding.

### Learning Objectives
By the end of this module, you should understand:
*   **Hyperfocus**.
*   **Environment Design**.
*   **Communication Styles**.

---

## 2. Core Content

### The Asset
*   **Pattern Recognition**: Seeing anomalies in logs that others miss.
*   **Hyperfocus**: Digging into a forensic artifact for 12 hours straight.
*   **Lateral Thinking**: Solving problems in non-linear ways.

### The Challenge
*   **Sensory Overload**: Open plan offices are kryptonite. (Noise cancelling headphones are PPE).
*   **Social Rituals**: Small talk can be draining. Focus on direct communication.

---

## 3. Guided Practice

### Accommodating the Team
**Scenario**: Brilliant analyst struggles with 9 AM meetings.
**Action**:
1.  Focus on Output, not Hours.
2.  Allow flexible schedules.
3.  Provide written instructions, not just verbal.

---

## 4. Reflection Check

1.  **Interviews**: Traditional "Culture Fit" interviews bias against neurodiversity. Use "Work Sample" tests instead.
2.  **Empathy**: Different is not broken. It's different.
3.  **Leadership**: Psychological safety allows these minds to bloom.

---

## 5. Completion Criteria

This module is complete when:
1.  You see **Neurodiversity** as a talent advantage.
2.  You adapt **Work Environments** for focus.
3.  You judge on **Output**, not conformity.
