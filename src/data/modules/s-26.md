# Geopolitics and Cyber Warfare

## 1. Orientation

### What this module covers
Cyber is the 5th domain of warfare. Attacks do not happen in a vacuum; they happen because of real-world conflict. This module connects **Geopolitics** (Real-world tension) with **Cyber Operations**.

### Learning Objectives
*   Analyze how kinetic war drives cyber activity.
*   Profile major Nation State actors (Russia, China, Iran, North Korea).
*   Understand **Hybrid Warfare**.

---

## 2. Core Content

### The Big Four (State Actors)
1.  **Russia (The Bear):**
    *   *Goal:* Disruption, Influence, Energy dominance.
    *   *Style:* Destructive (Wipers), NotPetya, Supply Chain (SolarWinds).
    *   *Sectors:* Energy, Government, Critical Infra.
2.  **China (The Panda):**
    *   *Goal:* IP Theft, Economic Advantage, Surveillance (Dissidents).
    *   *Style:* Massive scale database theft (Equifax, OPM).
    *   *Sectors:* Tech, Pharma, Defense Base.
3.  **Iran (The Kitten):**
    *   *Goal:* Retaliation, Regional dominance (Saudi/Israel).
    *   *Style:* Shamoon (Wiper), Ransomware-for-revenue.
4.  **North Korea (The Chollima):**
    *   *Goal:* Money (Funding the regime), Launch capability.
    *   *Style:* Bank Heists (SWIFT), Crypto theft, Sony Hack.

### Hybrid Warfare
Using cyber to soften a target before kinetic invasion.
*   *Ukraine 2022:* Wiper malware (WhisperGate) hit Ukraine Gov minutes before tanks crossed the border.
*   *Implication:* If political tension rises, raise cyber shields.
