# The First 90 Days (CISO Plan)

## 1. Orientation

### What this module covers
This module covers **Leadership Impact**. How to survive the start.

### Fit in the Learning Path
You got the job. Now what?

### Learning Objectives
By the end of this module, you should understand:
*   **Listening Tour**.
*   **Quick Wins**.
*   **The Baseline**.

---

## 2. Core Content

### Month 1: Listen
*   Don't change anything.
*   Meet stakeholders (HR, Legal, Product, Sales).
*   Ask: "What is the biggest risk?" and "How does Security slow you down?"

### Month 2: Evaluate
*   Run a baseline assessment (NIST CSF).
*   Find the skeletons in the closet.
*   Identify **Quick Wins** (e.g., Turn on MFA for Admins).

### Month 3: Strategy
*   Present the Roadmap to the Board.
*   Ask for the budget.

---

## 3. Guided Practice

### The Stakeholder Map
**Exercise**: Map the power.
*   *Supporters*: General Counsel (Needs you for compliance).
*   *Detractors*: VP Engineering (Thinks you slow them down).
*   *Strategy*: Convert the Detractor by solving a problem for them (e.g., "I can automate your compliance evidence").

---

## 4. Reflection Check

1.  **Arrogance**: Coming in and saying "Everything here sucks" is the fastest way to get fired. Respect the history.
2.  **Culture**: Culture eats strategy for breakfast. Learn the culture before enforcing rules.
3.  **Air Cover**: Get the CEO's backing early.

---

## 5. Completion Criteria

This module is complete when:
1.  You have a **90-Day Plan**.
2.  You identify **Key Stakeholders**.
3.  You deliver a **Quick Win**.
