# Hiring & Retention

## 1. Orientation

### What this module covers
This module covers **The Talent Shortage**.

### Fit in the Learning Path
You are only as good as your team.

### Learning Objectives
By the end of this module, you should understand:
*   **Job Descriptions**.
*   **The Unicorno Hunting**.
*   **Burnout Prevention**.

---

## 2. Core Content

### The Job Description
*   *Bad*: "Entry Level. Must have CISSP and 10 years experience."
*   *Good*: "Junior Analyst. Curiosity required. We will train you."

### Retention
*   Security people leave because:
    1.  Burnout (On-call hell).
    2.  Boredom (Doing the same ticket 100 times).
    3.  Management (Toxic culture).
*   *Fix*: Automate the boring stuff. Rotate duties. Pay well.

---

## 3. Guided Practice

### The Pipeline
**Strategy**: Don't hire Seniors. Hire Juniors and turn them into Seniors.
**Pools**:
1.  Internal Helpdesk (They know the network).
2.  Career Switchers.
3.  Veterans.

**Mentorship**: Pair a Senior with a Junior.

---

## 4. Reflection Check

1.  **Diversity**: Diverse teams catch more threats. (Groupthink is a vulnerability).
2.  **Training Budget**: If you don't train them, they leave. If you do train them, they might leave, but if you don't, they stay and are incompetent.
3.  **Remote Work**: Top talent wants remote flexibility.
